2022 Film Festival Dates

List of Film Festivals
Oct 30
Written By Mehdi Balamissa

We have rounded up the announced dates of the 2022 editions of the world’s main film festivals, including Cannes, Berlin, Sundance, Venice, Sarajevo, SXSW, IDFA…

As Film Fest Report pursues its mission to promote independent cinema and report from the film festival circuit towards our engaged audience of filmmakers, film professionals and film enthusiasts; we round up, every month, the most extensive list of film festivals taking place, as possible.

Feel free to reach out to us to flag your festival so we include it in our listing. And make sure to explore how we can promote your film festival, by having a look at our coverage opportunities (on-site coverage, interviews, …).
