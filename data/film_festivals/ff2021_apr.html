April 2021: List of Film Festivals

List of Film Festivals
Mar 26
Written By Film Fest Report

Discover the list of film festivals happening in April 2021. Hong Kong, Hot Docs, Atlanta Film Festival, Seattle International Film Festival, CPH:DOX and many more! Whether they organize in-person, virtual or hybrid editions, they are around the corner.

Film Fest Report is a digital media reporting on the film festival circuit all year round. We support and promote festivals of all kinds (Sundance, SXSW, Berlinale, IDFA…), anywhere (USA, Finland, Germany, Sweden, Italy…), towards our engaged audience of filmmakers, film enthusiasts and film programmers. Make sure to explore partnership opportunities to promote your event! On top of reviewing films, conducting interviews with filmmakers and programmers, each month, we publish an extensive list of film festivals (March 2021, February 2021…).

Below is the list of film festivals happening in April 2021. Add them to your calendar!
